Anaemic Model

Word

Anemic, US => Anaemic, BR /əˈniːmɪk

What it refers

When you implement a domain model with logic-less data values, and you put all business logic about them inside services, your model is a anaemic-model. In fact in this case your value/object is week, meaning that it is so passive and doesn't represent any logic of the bussines.
